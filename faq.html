Frequently Asked Questions

Getting to TAEHS!

We will be at Thomas A. Edison CTE HS's Cafeteria and Library at 165-65 84th Avenue, Jamaica, New York 11432. Enter through the main entrance. Jamaica, Queens is accessible via the E, J, Z, and F train within a 30 minute walk of the station.

Arrival Time

Doors will open at 9AM and the event will end at 5PM. Arrive from 9-10AM.

What to Bring?

Please bring your laptops, chargers, personal snacks, and yourselves obviously. This is a day event, so no need for extras that you would bring to an overnight style hackathon. Just bring what you need to work on a sprig game(just a laptop), or any other kind of game you plan on making.

Who's eligible to come?

All High School students will be welcome to participate and compete. Those not yet in high school can contact us for a wavier of this. NYC High School Students we welcome you to Edison.

Cost to Join?

We are going to be a free event. There have been suggestions to charge an entrance fee for how much we need funding to make this happen. We won't. All the high school events that Sahib and Kyle have been to have been free events. The mission is to provide an unforgettable experience to students. We want students of all different backgrounds to get the opportunities we have been afforded by traveling to different hackathons. We don't want to make cost a consideration on that principle.

Food?

We will provide snacks and food(*it's pizza*). TAEHS serves a diverse range of students with various dietary restrictions. Pizza is the best we can do.

Time

The hackathon style Game Jam will open doors at 10AM and close out at 6PM.

What We are Doing!

In the hours we are operating, you will get the chance to work in teams on games using sprig or other means in a mild competitive setting. There will be prizes for certain categories that we will announce.
