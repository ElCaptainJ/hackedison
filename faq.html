Frequently Asked Questions

Getting to TAEHS!

We will be at Thomas A. Edison CTE HS's Cafeteria and Library at 165-65 84th Avenue, Jamaica, New York 11432. Enter through the main entrance. Jamaica, Queens is accessible via the E, J, Z, and F train within a 30 minute walk of the station.

Time

Doors will open at 9AM and the event will end at 5PM. Arrive from 9-10AM.

What to Bring

Please bring your laptops, chargers, personal snacks, and yourselves. This is a day event so pack lightly.

Who's eligible to come

All High School students will be welcome to participate and compete. Those not yet in high school can contact us for a wavier of this. NYC High School Students we welcome you to Edison.

Cost to Join

We are going to be a free event. We think it's an important precedent to uphold.

Food

We will provide snacks, drinks, and pizza.

Prizes

Prizes will be announced on our instagram page.

What We are Doing!

In the hours we are operating, you will get the chance to work in teams on games using sprig or other means in a mild competitive setting. There will be prizes for certain categories that we will announce on our instagram.

How to Help?

Help spread the word about our first event. It would help us have a diverse group of people joining, along with gaining connections with potential sponsors.

Donating

Donations help us improve the quality of opportunities for our members. We are a fiscally sponsored organization and donations are tax deductible. Link for Donations

Why should you come

Game Jams and Hackathons are a great opportunity to meet new people, learn new skills, and gain access to opportunities that you wouldn't have access to in the future. If you're in doubt, feel free to contact us.

What is Sprig

Sprig is a custom handheld console using a custom javascript runtime enabling you to write 2d tile based javascript games which is ideal for beginners.
